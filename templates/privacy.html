Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when you create an account, along with the expense records you log — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect basic technical information, such as your browser type and usage activity, to help us maintain and improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide core features of the Service, such as recording your expenses, generating spending summaries, and keeping your account secure. Your information may also be used to communicate with you about your account, respond to support requests, and improve the functionality and reliability of Spendly. We do not use your expense data for advertising purposes.

3. Storage

Your account details and expense records are stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, after which your information will be permanently removed from our systems within a reasonable period.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may rely on trusted third-party providers for functions such as hosting, authentication, and analytics. These providers only have access to the information necessary to perform their services and are required to protect your data in line with this Privacy Policy. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website. We're happy to help with any privacy-related questions or requests.
